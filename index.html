Hi, I am Milin Zhang (张 敉霖), a 3rd-year PhD Candidate at Northeastern University, working in the Institute for the Wireless Internet of Things (WIoT), advised by Francesco Restuccia. I received my M.S. in electrical engineering at Syracuse University in 2021. I received B.S. at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2018.
I'm currently focusing on resilient and thrust-worthy AI/ML in resource constrained scenarios such as mobile edge computing. in addition, I work on AI-enpowered wireless communication/networking such as spectrum sensing and integreted sensing and communication for next-generation wireless technologies.

Research Interest:
  • Thrust-Worthy Machine Learning

  • AI/ML in Edge Computing

  • AI-driven Wireless Network

  • Integreted Sensing and Communication
